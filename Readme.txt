DESIGN PRINCIPLE VS DESIGN PATTERN :

--->Design principles are provided with some high-level guidelines to make the software designs
more understandable, flexible, maintainable.They do not provide any implementation and also t-
hey are not bound to any programming language. (e.g. Solid principles)

_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-_-

--->Design patterns are reusable solutions to problems we encounter in programming.They are ba-
sically used to solve the problems of "object generation" and "object integration".
